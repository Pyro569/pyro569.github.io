TSA

TSA is the Technology Student Association. You can imagine TSA as an engineering club but you compete against other nerdy high schoolers to win trophies or awards at competitions to show your dominance in your technology skills. My tenure in TSA so far is 3 years, starting in my freshman year of high school. I joined TSA relatively late into the TSA year (right before our state competition) and did the cybersecurity event and placed in the top three, thus going to TSA Nationals 2022 in Dallas, Texas. After that year my participation in TSA expanded massively, deciding to take on more events and projects.
