Timewinder

Timewinder is KobiWare Software Solution's second video game release, while it was not as large of a hit as Stranded in the Sewers, it was still a major hit, especially within the crowd that enjoys puzzle games. Set 2000 years in the future you play as John-E-VI, a time travelling robot who is stuck in a rundown facility. Utilizing the power of your time travelling you must escape from this facility.
