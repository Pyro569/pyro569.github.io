Timewinder

Timewinder was made in 2023 for the Kentucky TSA State Video Game Design event. The game was made over a period of about three weeks and did not place at TSA State but was however given the chance to be entered in TSA Nationals 2023 in Louisville, Kentucky and placed number 12 nation wide for the Video Game Design Event. The full game can be played here. Source code to the game can also be viewed on github.
